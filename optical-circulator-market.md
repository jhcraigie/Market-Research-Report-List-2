<p><p>The Optical Circulator market includes two main types: Single-Mode Type and Polarization-Maintaining Type. The Single-Mode Type circulator is designed for single-mode fibers, allowing for the transmission of light through a single pathway. On the other hand, the Polarization-Maintaining Type circulator is specifically designed to maintain the polarization of light traveling through the device, making it ideal for applications where polarization control is critical. These different types cater to specific needs and requirements within the optical communication industry.</p></p>
