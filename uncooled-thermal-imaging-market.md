<p><p>Uncooled Thermal Imaging technology is categorized based on the wavelength of infrared radiation it detects. Short Wave IR, Mid Wave IR, Long Wave IR, and Far Wave IR markets represent different segments within the thermal imaging industry. Short Wave IR detects wavelengths between 0.9 to 1.5 microns, Mid Wave IR detects between 3 to 5 microns, Long Wave IR detects between 7 to 14 microns, and Far Wave IR detects beyond 14 microns. Each market type has its unique applications and benefits in various industries like military, security, surveillance, and automotive.</p></p>
