<p><strong>The Wood Repair Epoxy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>The Liquid Version</li><li>The Putty Version</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wood repair epoxy comes in two main types - the liquid version and the putty version. The liquid version is ideal for filling in cracks and small gaps, as well as for sealing and reinforcing wood fibers. The putty version, on the other hand, is better suited for larger repairs, such as filling in holes or rebuilding missing chunks of wood. Both types are easy to use and provide a strong, durable bond when fully cured, making them popular choices for DIY wood repair projects.</p></p>
