<p><p>Optical liquid point level sensors are available in two main types: contact and contactless. Contact sensors make direct contact with the liquid to detect its level, while contactless sensors use technology such as infrared or ultrasonic waves to sense the liquid level without the need for physical contact. Contact sensors are typically more cost-effective and easier to install, while contactless sensors offer benefits such as non-intrusive monitoring and reduced risk of contamination. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
