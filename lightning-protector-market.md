<p><strong>The Lightning Protector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voltage Switch Type</li><li>Pressure Limiting Type</li><li>Combination Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lightning Protector Market includes Voltage Switch Type, Pressure Limiting Type, and Combination Type protectors. Voltage switch type protectors work by diverting excess voltage to ground when a certain threshold is exceeded. Pressure limiting type protectors limit the build-up of excessive voltage by dissipating it as heat. Combination type protectors combine the features of both voltage switch and pressure limiting types, offering a comprehensive protection solution against lightning strikes and power surges. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
