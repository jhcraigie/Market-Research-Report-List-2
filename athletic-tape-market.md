<p><p>Athletic tape market consists of two main types: roll tape and pre-cut bandages. Roll tape is a continuous length of tape that can be cut to the desired length for specific applications. This type of tape allows for customization and flexibility in wrapping joints and securing equipment. Pre-cut bandages, on the other hand, are pre-shaped and sized for specific body parts or injuries. They are convenient for quick and easy application without the need for cutting or measuring. Both types serve the purpose of providing support and protection during physical activity.</p></p>
