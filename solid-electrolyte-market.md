<p><p>Solid electrolytes are crucial components in batteries as they conduct ions between the cathode and anode. Inorganic solid electrolytes are made from materials like ceramics and glass, offering high conductivity and stability. Composite solid electrolytes combine inorganic and organic materials for improved performance. Polymer solid electrolytes consist of polymer chains with embedded ions, offering flexibility and safety. Each type of solid electrolyte has unique properties and characteristics that make them suitable for different battery applications.</p></p>
