<p><strong>The Automotive Forgings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Die Forgings</li><li>Open Die Forgings</li><li>Rolled Rings Forgings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive forgings market consists of three main types: Closed Die Forgings, Open Die Forgings, and Rolled Rings Forgings. Closed Die Forgings involve pressing or hammering metal into a specific shape within a set mold. Open Die Forgings are formed by manipulating heated metal between flat dies, allowing for more intricate shapes. Rolled Rings Forgings are created by shaping metal into a seamless ring shape through radial and axial pressure. Each type of forging method offers unique applications and benefits within the automotive industry.</p></p>
