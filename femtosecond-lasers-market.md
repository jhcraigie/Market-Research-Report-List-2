<p><strong>The Femtosecond Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Titanium-sapphire Lasers</li><li>Diode-pumped Lasers</li><li>Fiber Lasers</li><li>Mode-locked Diode Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Femtosecond lasers are categorized into four main types: titanium-sapphire lasers, diode-pumped lasers, fiber lasers, and mode-locked diode lasers. Titanium-sapphire lasers utilize a crystal to generate ultra-short pulses. Diode-pumped lasers use semiconductor diodes to pump the gain medium. Fiber lasers utilize optical fibers for amplification. Mode-locked diode lasers achieve ultra-short pulses through mode-locking. Each type of femtosecond laser has unique advantages and applications, making them valuable tools in various industries such as medical, scientific research, and manufacturing.</p></p>
