<p><p>Magnetic powder cores are widely used in electrical and electronic devices for their high efficiency and reliability. The market is segmented into different types based on their composition and properties. MPP (Molypermalloy Powder) cores offer high saturation flux density and low core loss. Sendust cores provide a balance between high saturation flux density and good frequency characteristics. High flux cores have the highest saturation flux density among all types. Fe-Si cores are composed of iron-silicon powder and offer good magnetic properties at high frequencies.</p></p>
