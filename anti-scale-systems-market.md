<p><strong>The Anti-Scale Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Softeners</li><li>Reverse Osmosis</li><li>Ion Exchange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-Scale Systems are used to prevent the build-up of scale in water-based systems. There are three main types of Anti-Scale Systems in the market: Softeners, Reverse Osmosis, and Ion Exchange. Softeners remove calcium and magnesium ions that cause hardness in water. Reverse Osmosis uses a semi-permeable membrane to remove impurities and mineral ions from water. Ion Exchange systems replace hardness ions with sodium ions. Each type of system has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications depending on the level of hardness in the water.</p></p>
