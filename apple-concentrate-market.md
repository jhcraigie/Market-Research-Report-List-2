<p><p>Apple concentrate market consists of various types including puree concentrate, clear concentrate, juice concentrate, powdered concentrate, and frozen concentrate. Puree concentrate is made by grounding the whole apple into a puree consistency. Clear concentrate is made by removing impurities and water from apple juice. Juice concentrate is made by extracting water from apple juice. Powdered concentrate is made by dehydrating apple juice into a powder form. Frozen concentrate is made by freezing apple juice into a concentrated form for later use.</p></p>
