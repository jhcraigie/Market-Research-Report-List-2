<p><p>Disposable Breathing Filters are used to remove bacteria, viruses, and particulate matter from the air being inhaled by patients in medical settings. There are two main types of filters - Mechanical Filters and Electrostatic Filters. Mechanical filters work by physically trapping particles, while electrostatic filters use an electric charge to attract and capture particles. Both types are used in various medical devices such as ventilators, anesthesia machines, and respiratory therapy equipment to ensure clean and safe air for patients.</p></p>
