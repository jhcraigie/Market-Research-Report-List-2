<p><ul><li>Structured Light</li><li>Time of Flight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Structured Light and Time of Flight are two types of 3D sensing technology used in various industries. Structured Light works by projecting a pattern of light onto an object and measuring the distortion of the pattern to create a 3D image. Time of Flight, on the other hand, works by emitting light pulses and calculating the time it takes for the light to reflect back, providing accurate depth information. Both technologies have their unique applications in fields such as augmented reality, robotics, and biometrics.</p></p>
