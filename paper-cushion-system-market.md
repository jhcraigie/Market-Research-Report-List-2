<p><p>Paper cushion systems are a type of packaging solution used to protect products during shipping. There are three main types of paper cushion systems on the market: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual systems require the user to tear and crumple paper by hand, while semi-automatic systems involve a machine that assists in the process. Automatic systems are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention. Each type offers varying levels of efficiency and convenience, catering to different packaging needs.</p></p>
