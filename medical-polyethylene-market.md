<p><p>Medical polyethylene is a widely used material in the healthcare industry due to its durability, flexibility, and chemical resistance. There are two main types of medical polyethylene available in the market: LDPE (low-density polyethylene) and HDPE (high-density polyethylene). LDPE is commonly used for medical packaging, tubing, and prosthetic implants due to its flexibility and impact resistance. On the other hand, HDPE is preferred for medical devices and equipment requiring high strength, rigidity, and chemical resistance such as surgical trays and bottles.</p></p>
