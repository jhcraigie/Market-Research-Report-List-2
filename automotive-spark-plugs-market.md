<p><ul><li>Hot Spark Plugs</li><li>Cold Spark Plugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive spark plugs can be categorized into two main types: hot spark plugs and cold spark plugs. Hot spark plugs are designed to maintain a higher operating temperature, which helps burn off carbon deposits and prevent fouling in engines that operate at lower temperatures. On the other hand, cold spark plugs are designed to dissipate heat more effectively, making them suitable for engines that operate at higher temperatures or under heavy loads. Each type of spark plug is optimized for specific engine conditions to ensure optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
