<p><strong>The Medium Voltage Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Type</li><li>Oil Immersed Type</li><li>VPI (Vacuum Pressure Impregnated Type)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medium Voltage Transformers are classified into three main types based on their insulation material and method: </p><p>1. Dry Type Transformers are insulated with epoxy resin and are suitable for indoor applications where fire safety is a concern. </p><p>2. Oil Immersed Type Transformers use mineral oil as the insulating medium and are commonly used in outdoor applications due to their high reliability and efficiency. </p><p>3. VPI (Vacuum Pressure Impregnated) Type Transformers utilize a process where the windings are vacuum-sealed with epoxy for enhanced insulation and thermal conductivity.</p></p>
