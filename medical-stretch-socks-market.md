<p><p>Medical stretch socks market consists of two main types - gradient socks and anti-embolism socks. Gradient socks apply varying levels of pressure on the legs to improve circulation and reduce swelling. These are commonly used by individuals with circulation issues or who stand for long periods. Anti-embolism socks, on the other hand, are specifically designed to prevent blood clots during surgery or periods of immobility. Both types are important in managing leg health and preventing complications such as deep vein thrombosis.</p></p>
