<p><p>The Synthetic Cutting Oil Market can be categorized into two main types: Water Base Cutting Oil and Oil Base Cutting Oil. Water Base Cutting Oil is a type of synthetic cutting oil that is water soluble, environmentally friendly, and provides excellent cooling properties during the machining process. On the other hand, Oil Base Cutting Oil is oil soluble and offers superior lubrication properties, making it ideal for heavy-duty machining operations. Both types of cutting oils are popular choices in the manufacturing industry for their ability to improve tool life and enhance machining accuracy.</p></p>
