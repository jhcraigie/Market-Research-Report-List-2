<p><ul><li>Monorail</li><li>Double Track</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car lift rail market is divided into two main types: Monorail and Double Track. Monorail systems consist of a single rail track where the lift carriage travels along, offering a more compact and streamlined design. On the other hand, Double Track systems feature two rail tracks where the carriage moves between them, allowing for more stability and greater weight capacity. Both types are commonly used in industrial settings and warehouses for efficiently transporting vehicles or heavy loads vertically.</p></p>
