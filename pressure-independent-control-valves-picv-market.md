<p><p>Pressure Independent Control Valves (PICV) can be categorized into two main market types: Thread PICV and Flange PICV. Thread PICV valves are typically smaller in size and are designed to be directly screwed onto piping systems. Meanwhile, Flange PICV valves are larger and are installed using flanges for better connection and stability. Both types of PICV valves offer precise flow control and maintain constant flow rates regardless of pressure fluctuations, making them ideal for HVAC systems and other applications requiring accurate and efficient control of fluid flow.</p></p>
