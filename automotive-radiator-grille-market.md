<p><p>Automotive radiator grilles come in three main types - horizontal grille, vertical grille, and mesh grille. The horizontal grille features bars running across the front of the vehicle, giving a sleek and wide appearance. The vertical grille has bars that run up and down, creating a more traditional and imposing look. The mesh grille is made up of a series of intertwined wires, offering a modern and sporty design. Each type of grille caters to different aesthetic preferences and can enhance the overall look of a vehicle.</p></p>
