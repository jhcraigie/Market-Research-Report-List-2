<p><p>Thermal conductive additives are materials added to polymers to enhance their thermal conductivity. There are two main types of thermal conductive additives market: thermoplastic additive market and thermosetting additive market. Thermoplastic additives are added to polymers that become pliable when heated and solid when cooled. Thermosetting additives are added to polymers that undergo a chemical reaction when heated, creating a tough and durable material. Both types of additives play a crucial role in improving the heat dissipation properties of polymers in various industrial applications.</p></p>
