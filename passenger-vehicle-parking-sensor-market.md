<p><p>Passenger Vehicle Parking Sensors help drivers to park their vehicles more easily and safely. There are two main types of parking sensors: Forward and Rear View. Forward parking sensors are installed at the front of the vehicle and help drivers to navigate tight spaces and avoid collisions. Rear View parking sensors are located at the back of the vehicle and alert drivers to obstacles behind them while parking. Both types of sensors use technology such as ultrasonic sensors to detect objects in the vehicle's path and provide audible and visual warnings to the driver.</p></p>
