<p><p>Room hygrostats market can be classified into single stage and double stage types. In single stage market, the hygrostat controls the humidity levels by simply turning on or off as needed. This type is more basic and straightforward. On the other hand, in double stage market, the hygrostat has two stages that allow for more precise control over humidity levels by adjusting the intensity of the system. This type is more advanced and ideal for applications where precise humidity control is required.</p></p>
