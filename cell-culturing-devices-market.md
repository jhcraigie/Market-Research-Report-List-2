<p><p>Cell culturing devices market can be categorized into two main types: cell model systems and cell integrated systems. Cell model systems are designed for culturing cells in a controlled environment to mimic in vivo conditions for research purposes. On the other hand, cell integrated systems incorporate additional components such as sensors, pumps, and filters to create a more comprehensive cell culture platform. Both types of devices play a crucial role in advancing research and development in fields such as drug discovery and regenerative medicine.</p></p>
