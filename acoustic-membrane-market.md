<p><p>The acoustic membrane market is dominated by two main types of materials, Polyurethane (PU) and Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE). PU membranes are known for their elastic properties and ability to absorb sound waves, making them ideal for applications where vibration damping and noise reduction are important. On the other hand, PTFE membranes are highly inert, durable and weather-resistant, making them suitable for outdoor applications where exposure to harsh conditions is a concern. Both types of membranes offer unique benefits and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
