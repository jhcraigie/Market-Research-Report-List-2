<p><ul><li>Thermostatic Mixing Valves</li><li>Thermostatic Diverting Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermostatic control valves are valves that automatically regulate temperature in a system to maintain a desired set point. Two main types of thermostatic control valves are thermostatic mixing valves and thermostatic diverting valves. Thermostatic mixing valves mix hot and cold water to achieve a set temperature, commonly used in showers. Thermostatic diverting valves control the flow of water between different outlets to maintain a set temperature, often used in radiant heating systems. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining comfort and safety in various applications.</p></p>
