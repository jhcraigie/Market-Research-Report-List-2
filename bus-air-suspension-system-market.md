<p><p>Bus air suspension systems come in two main types: Manual Air Suspension and Electronic Air Suspension. Manual air suspension requires the driver to manually adjust the air pressure in the system to control the ride height and comfort of the bus. On the other hand, Electronic air suspension uses sensors and electronic controls to automatically adjust the air pressure in real-time, providing a more advanced and customizable solution for optimizing ride quality and stability in buses. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
