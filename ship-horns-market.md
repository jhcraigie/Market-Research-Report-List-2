<p><p>Ship horns market is categorized into three major types based on the technology used - Pneumatic ship horns, Electric ship horns, and Digital ship horns. Pneumatic ship horns operate on compressed air, producing a loud sound signal. Electric ship horns use electricity to generate the sound signal, making them suitable for smaller vessels. Digital ship horns are equipped with advanced digital technology for precise control and customization of sound signals. Each type caters to different needs and requirements of ships in terms of signal range, power consumption, and functionality.</p></p>
