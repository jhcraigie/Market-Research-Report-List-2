<p><p>Manual ceramic tile cutters are available in two main types: single track and double track. Single track manual tile cutters have one linear track along which the cutting wheel moves to score the tile before breaking it. Double track manual tile cutters have two parallel tracks, allowing for easier and more precise scoring and breaking of tiles. Both types of manual ceramic tile cutters are commonly used in the construction industry for cutting and installing ceramic tiles.</p></p>
