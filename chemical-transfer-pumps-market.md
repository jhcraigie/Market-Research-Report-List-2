<p><p>Chemical transfer pumps come in various types to suit different needs. AC transfer pumps are powered by alternating current and are ideal for continuous and heavy-duty use. DC transfer pumps, on the other hand, run on direct current and are more portable and versatile, making them suitable for remote locations. Hand transfer pumps do not require any external power source and are manually operated, making them perfect for small-scale and occasional transfer tasks. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
