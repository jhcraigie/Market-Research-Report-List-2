<p><ul><li>Direct Chlorination (DC) Process</li><li>Hydrochlorinaton (HC) Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Grade Trichlorosilane market can be classified into two main types: the Direct Chlorination (DC) Process and the Hydrochlorinaton (HC) Process. In the DC process, trichlorosilane is produced by reacting silicon with hydrogen chloride gas, while in the HC process, trichlorosilane is produced by reacting silicon with chlorine gas and hydrogen gas. Both processes are widely used in the semiconductor industry for producing high-purity trichlorosilane, which is a key raw material in the production of silicon wafers.</p></p>
