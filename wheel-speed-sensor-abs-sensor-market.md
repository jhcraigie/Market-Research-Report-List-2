<p><p>The wheel speed sensor, also known as the ABS sensor, comes in two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors use a transistor to measure changes in magnetic fields, while Magnetic Electric Type sensors utilize a magnetic ring and a coil to measure wheel speed. Both types of sensors are crucial in modern vehicle braking systems to ensure optimal performance and safety by detecting wheel speed variations and sending signals to the ABS system.</p></p>
