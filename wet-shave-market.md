Manual shavers are the traditional razors that require a user to manually glide across the skin. Rotary electric shavers use circular blades that rotate in a circular motion. Reciprocating electric shavers have blades that move back and forth in a straight line motion. Each type of shaver caters to different preferences and needs of consumers, offering a variety of options for a comfortable and effective shave.</p></p>
