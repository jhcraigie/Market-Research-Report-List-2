<p><p>Freight railway infrastructure maintenance involves four main market types: track, signaling, civils, and other. Track maintenance focuses on the physical condition of the tracks, including repairs and maintenance to ensure safe and efficient operation. Signaling maintenance involves the maintenance of signaling systems to ensure proper communication and safety on the railway. Civils maintenance includes the upkeep of bridges, tunnels, and other structures along the railway line. The "other" market type encompasses various other aspects of railway infrastructure maintenance, such as electrification and IT systems.</p></p>
