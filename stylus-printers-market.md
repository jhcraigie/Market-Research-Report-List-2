<p><p>Stylus printers are available in various market types, including Ethernet stylus printers, USB stylus printers, and wireless stylus printers. Ethernet stylus printers are connected to a network through an Ethernet cable, allowing multiple users to access the printer. USB stylus printers are connected directly to a computer via a USB cable for individual use. Wireless stylus printers connect to devices through Wi-Fi or Bluetooth, offering convenience and flexibility in printing from multiple devices without the need for physical connections.</p></p>
