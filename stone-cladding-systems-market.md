<p><p>Stone cladding systems market consists of two main types: natural stone and technical stone. On the other hand, technical stone cladding includes manufactured stones like concrete, ceramic, and engineered quartz. Both types offer a wide range of options in terms of color, texture, and durability, making them popular choices for enhancing the aesthetic appeal and structural integrity of buildings.</p></p>
