<p><p>Bone Densitometer Systems are used to measure bone density and diagnose osteoporosis. There are two main types of Bone Densitometer Systems in the market - Axial Bone Densitometer and Peripheral Bone Densitometer. Axial Bone Densitometers measure bone density in the spine and hip, providing highly accurate results. Peripheral Bone Densitometers, on the other hand, measure bone density in the peripheral skeleton like the wrist, heel or finger, offering a more convenient and portable option for screening osteoporosis. Both types of systems play a crucial role in assessing bone health and preventing fractures.</p></p>
