<p><p>Drywall panels come in various types to meet different needs. Regular drywall panels are the standard option for most construction projects. Mold resistant drywall panels are designed to prevent mold growth in areas with high humidity. Moisture resistant drywall panels are ideal for wet areas like bathrooms and kitchens. Fire resistant drywall panels provide added protection against fire damage.
