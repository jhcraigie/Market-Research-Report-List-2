<p><p>The denitration market can be categorized into three main types: Cellular Type, Plate Type, and Corrugated Type. Cellular Type denitrators consist of a honeycomb structure to promote contact between the denitrification agent and the gas stream. Plate Type denitrators have flat plates stacked together to facilitate the removal of nitrogen oxides. Corrugated Type denitrators have a corrugated surface to increase the surface area for better nitrogen oxide removal. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in industrial processes.</p></p>
