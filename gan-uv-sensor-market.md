<p><p>GaN UV sensors are widely used in various markets, including UVA, UVB, and UVC sensor markets. UVA sensors specifically detect ultraviolet rays with wavelengths between 315-400nm, commonly used in skin care products and scientific applications. UVB sensors detect rays in the 280-315nm range, vital for monitoring UV exposure in environmental and consumer electronics. UVC sensors detect shorter wavelengths below 280nm, crucial for sterilization and disinfection applications in healthcare, water treatment, and food safety industries. Each market type serves specific purposes in different industries.</p></p>
