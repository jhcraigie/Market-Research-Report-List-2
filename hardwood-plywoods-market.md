<p><p>Hardwood plywoods are categorized into three main market types based on their thickness: below 6mm, 6mm-18mm, and above 18mm. Plywoods below 6mm are often used for decorative purposes or in applications where weight is a factor. Plywoods between 6mm and 18mm are versatile and commonly used in furniture, cabinetry, and construction. Plywoods above 18mm are thick and heavy-duty, suitable for structural applications such as subfloors and heavy-duty furniture. Each market type caters to specific needs and requirements in various industries.</p></p>
