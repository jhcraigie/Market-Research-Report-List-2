<p><strong>The Diode Heat Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Trap Diodes</li><li>Vapor Trap Diodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diode Heat Pipes are categorized into two main types: Liquid Trap Diodes and Vapor Trap Diodes. Liquid Trap Diodes utilize a wick structure to facilitate the movement of liquid from the evaporator section to the condenser section, while Vapor Trap Diodes rely on the movement of vapor to transfer heat. Both types of heat pipes are effective in dissipating heat efficiently and are commonly used in various applications such as electronic devices, aerospace systems, and thermal management solutions.</p></p>
