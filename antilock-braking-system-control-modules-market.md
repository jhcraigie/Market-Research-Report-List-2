<p><ul><li>One-channel ABS</li><li>Two-channel ABS</li><li>Three-channel ABS</li><li>Four-channel ABS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antilock Braking System Control Modules are categorized into different types based on the number of channels they control. One-channel ABS systems operate only one brake circuit, typically found in motorcycles. Two-channel ABS systems control two brake circuits, commonly used in passenger cars. Three-channel ABS systems manage three brake circuits, often seen in trucks with rear-wheel ABS. Four-channel ABS systems regulate all four brake circuits individually, commonly found in modern vehicles, providing improved stability and control during braking.</p></p>
