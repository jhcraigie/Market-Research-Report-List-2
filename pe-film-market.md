<p><p>PE Film market is comprised of various types such as LLDPE (Linear Low Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low Density Polyethylene), and HDPE (High Density Polyethylene). LLDPE is known for its high tensile strength and puncture resistance, making it suitable for packaging applications. LDPE is flexible and excellent for moisture barrier properties, making it ideal for food packaging. HDPE is rigid and has good chemical resistance, making it perfect for industrial applications like construction and automotive industries. Each type caters to different market needs and applications.</p></p>
