<p><p>Cork gaskets are primarily made from three main materials: Neoprene Cork, Nitrile Cork, and Composite Cork. Neoprene Cork gaskets are known for their oil and fuel-resistant properties, making them ideal for automotive applications. Nitrile Cork gaskets offer excellent chemical resistance and are commonly used in industrial settings. Composite Cork gaskets are a blend of cork and rubber, providing a balance of flexibility and durability. Each material caters to different needs in the market based on their specific properties and applications.</p></p>
