<p><strong>The Automated External Defibrillator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-automated</li><li>Fully automated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated External Defibrillator (AED) market includes two types: Semi-automated and Fully automated devices. Semi-automated AED require the user to deliver the shock by pressing a button after analyzing the heart rhythm, whereas Fully automated AED deliver the shock automatically without any user intervention. Semi-automated AED are often preferred in settings where trained personnel are present, while Fully automated AED are commonly used in public spaces where quick intervention is crucial and individuals may not have medical training.</p></p>
