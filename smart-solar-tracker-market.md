<p><p>Single Axis Solar Tracker market focuses on systems that allow solar panels to move along one axis, typically east to west, maximizing solar exposure throughout the day. This design is cost-effective and efficient for smaller installations. Dual Axis Solar Tracker market involves systems that allow solar panels to move along two axes, both east-west and north-south, maximizing solar exposure throughout the day and throughout the year. These systems are more expensive but offer higher energy production and efficiency. Both markets cater to the growing demand for renewable energy solutions.</p></p>
