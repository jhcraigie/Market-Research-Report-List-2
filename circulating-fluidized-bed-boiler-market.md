<p><strong>The Circulating Fluidized Bed Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subcritical</li><li>Supercritical</li><li>Ultra-supercritical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Circulating Fluidized Bed Boiler market is segmented into three types based on steam pressure levels: Subcritical, Supercritical, and Ultra-supercritical. Subcritical boilers operate at pressures below the critical point of water, Supercritical operate above the critical pressure, and Ultra-supercritical operate at even higher pressures. Subcritical boilers are cost-effective and widely used, whereas Supercritical and Ultra-supercritical boilers are more efficient and environmentally friendly due to higher steam temperatures and pressures. The market for each type varies based on the specific needs and regulations of different regions.</p></p>
