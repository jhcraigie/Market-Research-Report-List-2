<p><p>Copper paste is available in three main types - Low Temperature Sintered, Medium Temperature Sintered, and High Temperature Sintered. The Low Temperature Sintered paste is used for applications where a lower temperature is required for sintering. The Medium Temperature Sintered paste offers a balance between temperature and performance. The High Temperature Sintered paste is designed for applications that require high temperatures for sintering. Each type of copper paste caters to specific sintering requirements and provides varying levels of performance and durability.</p></p>
