<p><p>Serum Free Cell Freezing Medium is available in two main types: with DMSO and without DMSO. DMSO (dimethyl sulfoxide) is a cryoprotectant commonly used to prevent ice crystal formation during cell freezing. Cell freezing medium with DMSO is suitable for preserving a wide range of cell types, while DMSO-free medium is preferred for certain sensitive cell lines or applications where DMSO may interfere with downstream assays. The choice between the two types depends on the specific cell type and experimental requirements.</p></p>
