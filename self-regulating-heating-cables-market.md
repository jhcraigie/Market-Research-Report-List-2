<p><ul><li>CABT</li><li>CAMT</li><li>CAHT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self regulating heating cables are used in various industries to prevent freezing of pipelines and tanks. The CABT (Constant Wattage, Area, Base Temperature) market type provides a constant output of heat regardless of temperature changes. CAMT (Constant Wattage, Area, Maintained Temperature) cables maintain a specific temperature in the area they cover. CAHT (Constant Wattage, Ambient Temperature, High Temperature) cables are designed to withstand high temperatures in ambient environments. Each type caters to different heating requirements in industrial settings.</p></p>
