<p><p>Non-Skid coatings are available in two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based coatings are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and have low VOC content, making them suitable for indoor use. On the other hand, solvent-based coatings offer higher durability and are more resistant to chemicals and abrasion, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Both types provide excellent non-slip properties, enhancing safety in various settings such as industrial facilities, commercial buildings, and residential spaces.</p></p>
