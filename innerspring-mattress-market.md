<p><p>In the innerspring mattress market, there are two main types: double-sided and single-sided mattresses. Double-sided innerspring mattresses have coils and support systems on both sides, allowing for flipping and rotation to prolong the mattress's lifespan and prevent sagging. Single-sided innerspring mattresses have coils and support systems on only one side, making them lighter and easier to maneuver, but they cannot be flipped. Consumers can choose between the two based on their preferences for durability and convenience.</p></p>
