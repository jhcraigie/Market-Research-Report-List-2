<p><p>Cold cast elastomers are materials that combine the benefits of plastic, rubber, and metal. The market can be divided into two types: two-component and three-component systems. Two-component systems involve mixing two components together to form a polymer, while three-component systems involve adding a third component, such as a curing agent. Two-component systems are simpler and have faster curing times, while three-component systems offer greater flexibility in terms of properties and performance. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
