<p><strong>The 12 Channel Electrocardiograph Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting ECG</li><li>Stress ECG</li><li>Holter ECG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>12 Channel Electrocardiograph Market includes three main types: Resting ECG, Stress ECG, and Holter ECG. Resting ECG is used to record the heart's electrical activity while the patient is at rest. Stress ECG involves monitoring heart function during physical exertion or stress. Holter ECG is a continuous recording of the heart's electrical activity over a period of time, usually 24-48 hours. Each type serves different purposes in diagnosing and monitoring heart conditions, contributing to the overall growth of the ECG market.</p></p>
