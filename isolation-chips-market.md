<p><p>Isolation chips are electronic components used to isolate or separate different parts of a circuit to prevent interference or damage. There are three main types of isolation chips: optocouplers, magnetic couplers, and capacitive couplers. Optocouplers use light to transfer signals between isolated sections of a circuit, magnetic couplers use magnetic fields for isolation, and capacitive couplers use electric fields. Each type has its unique benefits and applications in various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
