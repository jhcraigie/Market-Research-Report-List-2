<p><p>Digital X-ray devices market can be categorized into two main types: Retrofit X-ray Systems and New Digital X-ray Systems. Retrofit X-ray Systems involve upgrading traditional film-based X-ray machines to digital technology, allowing healthcare facilities to modernize their imaging capabilities without replacing the entire system. On the other hand, New Digital X-ray Systems refer to fully integrated digital X-ray machines that combine imaging hardware with advanced software for superior image quality and efficiency. Both types cater to the increasing demand for digital imaging solutions in healthcare.</p></p>
