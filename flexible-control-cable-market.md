<p><p>Flexible control cables are widely used in various industries for signal transmission and control purposes. The two main types of flexible control cables are shielded cables and unshielded cables. </p><p>Shielded cables are designed with a protective layer to minimize electromagnetic interference and provide high levels of signal integrity and reliability. </p><p>On the other hand, unshielded cables do not have this protective layer, making them more cost-effective but potentially more susceptible to interference. Both types of cables offer different benefits and are chosen based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
