<p><p>Mechanical palletizers come in two main types: low level and high level. Low level palletizers are closer to the ground and are typically used for applications with lower throughput requirements. They are more cost-effective and easier to integrate into existing production lines. High level palletizers, on the other hand, are taller machines that are used for high-speed and high-capacity applications. They are ideal for industries with large-scale production needs and require less manual labor for operation. Both types offer efficient palletizing solutions tailored to different production settings.</p></p>
