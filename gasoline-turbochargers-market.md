<p><p>Gasoline Turbochargers Market offers various types to cater to different needs. Variable Geometry Turbo Chargers (VGT/VNT) provide better engine response and improved efficiency by adjusting the angle of the turbine vanes. Waste Gate Turbo Chargers regulate boost pressure by diverting exhaust around the turbine. Twin Turbo Chargers use two turbochargers, one for low speeds and the other for high speeds, to deliver increased power and performance. Each type has its unique advantages, making them popular choices in the market.</p></p>
