<p><p>Fluid coils are commonly used in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems to transfer heat from a fluid to the surrounding air. There are two main types of fluid coils: tubed fluid coils and finned fluid coils. Tubed fluid coils have a series of tubes through which the fluid flows, while finned fluid coils have fins attached to the tubes to increase the surface area for better heat transfer. Both types are essential components in HVAC systems for efficient heat exchange.</p></p>
