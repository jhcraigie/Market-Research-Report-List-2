<p><ul><li>Sinker EDM</li><li>Wire EDM</li><li>Hole Drilling EDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) utilizes electrical discharges to remove material from a workpiece. The market for EDM is divided into three main types: Sinker EDM, Wire EDM, and Hole Drilling EDM. Sinker EDM uses a machined electrode to create cavities in the workpiece. Wire EDM involves moving a thin wire through the workpiece to cut it. Hole Drilling EDM is used to create precise holes in the workpiece. Each type of EDM caters to specific machining needs and applications.</p></p>
