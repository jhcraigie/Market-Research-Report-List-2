<p><p>Single wall, double wall, and triple wall corrugated boxes are types of multi-depth boxes used for packaging and shipping. Single wall boxes have one layer of corrugated material, double wall boxes have two layers, and triple wall boxes have three layers. Single wall boxes are lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for light to medium-weight items. Double wall boxes provide extra strength and are ideal for heavier items or items needing extra protection. Triple wall boxes offer the highest level of durability and are used for heavy-duty applications or for items requiring maximum protection during shipping.</p></p>
