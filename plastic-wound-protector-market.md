<p><ul><li>Ring Based</li><li>Prong Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic wound protectors come in two main types: ring-based and prong-based. Ring-based protectors feature a circular design that fits around the wound area, creating a barrier to prevent contamination and promote healing. On the other hand, prong-based protectors have small protrusions that extend outward from the center, providing additional protection and stability around the wound. Both types are commonly used in medical settings to protect surgical wounds and incisions from infection and other complications.</p></p>
