<p><p>Gas turbine engine market consists of various types including turbojet engine, turbofan engine, turbo propeller, and turbine shaft engine. Turbojet engine utilizes a simple design with high speeds and efficiency. Turbofan engine features a bypass duct to improve fuel efficiency and reduce noise. Turbo propeller engine uses a propeller to produce thrust, suitable for smaller aircraft. Turbine shaft engine drives a shaft to power propellers or rotors. Each type serves specific purposes in the aviation industry, catering to different requirements of aircraft.</p></p>
