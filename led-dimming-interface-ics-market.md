Step-up Driver ICs are designed to increase the voltage level from a power source to properly drive LED strings, while Step-down Driver ICs are meant to lower the voltage level to ensure efficient dimming of LED lights. Both types of ICs play a crucial role in controlling the brightness of LED lights, making them essential components in LED lighting systems.</p></p>
