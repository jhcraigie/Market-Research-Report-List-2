<p><p>The flat automobile horn market consists of two main types: electronic horns and electromagnetic horns. Electronic horns produce sound by converting electrical energy into sound waves, providing a more modern and consistent tone. On the other hand, electromagnetic horns create sound through vibrations caused by an electromagnetic coil, offering a louder and more traditional horn sound. Both types of horns are commonly used in vehicles to alert others of danger or to communicate in various driving situations.</p></p>
