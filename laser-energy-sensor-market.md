<p><strong>The Laser Energy Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyroelectric</li><li>Photodiode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser energy sensors can be classified into two main types: pyroelectric sensors and photodiode sensors. Pyroelectric sensors detect laser energy through the generation of electrical charges when exposed to heat, making them suitable for measuring pulsed laser energy. On the other hand, photodiode sensors use a semiconductor material to convert light energy into an electrical signal, making them more suitable for continuous wave laser energy detection. Both types of sensors play crucial roles in various applications involving laser energy measurement and monitoring.</p></p>
