<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Plastic Products</li><li>Thermoplastic Plastic Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GFRP composites market can be divided into two main types based on the type of resin used in the manufacturing process - Thermosetting plastic products and Thermoplastic plastic products. Thermosetting plastic products involve resins that undergo a chemical reaction during the curing process, resulting in a rigid and durable material. On the other hand, Thermoplastic plastic products involve resins that soften when heated and harden when cooled, allowing for easier processing and reshaping. Both types offer unique properties and benefits for various applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and aerospace.</p></p>
