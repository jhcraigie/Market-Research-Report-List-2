<p><p>Temperature monitoring systems are broadly categorized into two main types: wireless systems and wired systems. Wireless systems allow for remote monitoring through the use of sensors that transmit data wirelessly to a central hub or mobile device. This provides flexibility and convenience for users to access temperature data from anywhere. On the other hand, wired systems require physical connections between sensors and monitoring devices, offering a more reliable and stable connection but limited flexibility in terms of mobility. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
