<p><p>The ANSI industrial pumps market offers two main types: Horizontal ANSI Pump and Vertical ANSI Pump. Horizontal ANSI pumps are designed for high flow applications and are ideal for pumping liquids with minimal solid content. Vertical ANSI pumps, on the other hand, are best suited for installations where space is limited, as they take up less floor space than horizontal pumps. Both types of pumps adhere to American National Standards Institute (ANSI) specifications, ensuring high quality and performance in industrial applications.</p></p>
