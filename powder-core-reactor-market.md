<p><p>Powder core reactors are widely used in power conversion, filtering, and energy storage applications. Non-shielded powder core reactors are preferred for applications where electromagnetic interference is not a concern, such as inductors for power supplies. On the other hand, shielded powder core reactors are designed to provide additional electromagnetic shielding, making them suitable for sensitive electronic equipment. Both types of reactors offer high efficiency and reliability, making them a popular choice in various industries for their performance and versatility.</p></p>
