<p><p>LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-Fired Ceramics) glass powder market consists of two main types: Low-K and High-K. Low-K glass powder is characterized by its low dielectric constant, making it ideal for applications requiring minimal signal loss and high-speed data transmission. On the other hand, High-K glass powder has a high dielectric constant, which enhances capacitive properties and enables efficient energy storage in electronic devices. Both types play a crucial role in the development of advanced electronic components and microsystems.</p></p>
