<p><strong>The Truck Hydraulic Retarder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Hydraulic Retarder</li><li>Tandem Hydraulic Retarder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parallel Hydraulic Retarder and Tandem Hydraulic Retarder are two types of truck hydraulic retarders used to assist in slowing down vehicles and preventing brake overheating. Parallel Hydraulic Retarders are mounted directly on the driveshaft and provide effective braking power without affecting the vehicle's transmission system. Tandem Hydraulic Retarders, on the other hand, are placed between the transmission and the driveshaft, allowing for more precise control over braking power distribution. Both types of retarders offer efficient braking solutions for trucks, ensuring safe and reliable operation on the road.</p></p>
