<p><p>Capacitive fingerprint reader technology is used in two main market types: area and swipe. Area fingerprint readers require a user to place their finger on a designated area on the sensor for authentication. This type of reader is commonly used in smartphones and laptops. Swipe fingerprint readers require the user to swipe their finger across the sensor for authentication. This type of reader is often used in access control systems and time attendance devices. Both area and swipe fingerprint readers offer secure and convenient biometric authentication solutions.</p></p>
