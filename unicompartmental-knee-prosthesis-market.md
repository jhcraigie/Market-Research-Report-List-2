<p><p>Unicompartmental knee prosthesis is available in two market types: cemented bone and non-cemented bone. In the cemented bone market, the prosthesis is fixed to the bone using bone cement, providing immediate stability. In contrast, the non-cemented bone market involves securing the prosthesis to the bone without the use of cement, allowing for bone ingrowth and a more natural bone-prosthesis interface. Both market types offer options for patients based on their individual needs and preferences.</p></p>
