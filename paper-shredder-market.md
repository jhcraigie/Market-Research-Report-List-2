<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Paper Shredder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strip-Cut</li><li>Cross-Cut</li><li>Micro-Cut</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paper shredders come in three main types: Strip-Cut, Cross-Cut, and Micro-Cut. Strip-Cut shredders create long strips of paper, which are generally the least secure option. Cross-Cut shredders cut paper into small confetti-like pieces, offering a higher level of security. Lastly, Micro-Cut shredders produce the smallest particles, providing the highest level of security. Each type caters to different security needs, with Strip-Cut being the least secure, Cross-Cut offering medium security, and Micro-Cut ensuring the highest level of protection.</p></p>
