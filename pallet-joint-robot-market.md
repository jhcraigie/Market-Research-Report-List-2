<p><p>Pallet Joint Robots are classified based on their degrees of freedom, including 2-Axis, 4-Axis, and Others. 2-Axis robots have two rotational joints, allowing for simple up-and-down and side-to-side movements. 4-Axis robots have an additional rotary joint, enabling more complex movements such as bending, twisting, and rotating. The category of Others includes robots with more than 4 degrees of freedom, offering even greater flexibility and dexterity for various applications in industries such as manufacturing, packaging, and assembly.</p></p>
