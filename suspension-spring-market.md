<p><ul><li>Coil Spring</li><li>Air Spring</li><li>Leaf Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suspension spring market consists of three main types: coil spring, air spring, and leaf spring. Coil springs are helical springs that compress and expand to absorb shock and provide vehicle stability. Air springs use compressed air to support the vehicle's weight and provide a smooth and adjustable ride. Leaf springs are made up of several layers of metal strips that flex to absorb shocks and distribute weight. Each type of suspension spring has different characteristics and benefits, catering to various vehicle types and sizes.</p></p>
