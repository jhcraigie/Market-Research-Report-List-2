<p><p>Rennet market types include animal-derived rennet, microbial rennet, and vegetable rennet. Animal-derived rennet is sourced from the stomach lining of calves, lambs, or goats. Microbial rennet is a vegetarian alternative produced using bacteria or fungi. Vegetable rennet is derived from plants like fig trees or thistles. Each type caters to different dietary preferences and restrictions, providing options for individuals seeking animal-free or vegetarian alternatives for cheese making. The diverse rennet market offers choices for various consumer needs and values.</p></p>
