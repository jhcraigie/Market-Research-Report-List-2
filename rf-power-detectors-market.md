<p><p>RF Power Detectors are used in various applications for measuring the power levels of radio frequency signals. There are two main types of RF Power Detectors - RMS Power Detectors and Non-RMS Power Detectors. RMS Power Detectors provide an accurate measurement of the Root Mean Square power level of a signal, making them ideal for applications requiring precise power measurements. On the other hand, Non-RMS Power Detectors offer a simpler measurement of the average power level of a signal, making them suitable for less demanding applications.</p></p>
