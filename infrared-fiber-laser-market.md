<p><p>The Infrared Fiber Laser market can be categorized into two main types: Continuous Type and Impulse Type. Continuous Type lasers emit a continuous beam of infrared light, making them ideal for applications requiring a constant output. On the other hand, Impulse Type lasers produce short bursts of infrared light at high energies, making them suitable for tasks that require high peak powers. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries such as telecommunications, military, medical, and research.</p></p>
