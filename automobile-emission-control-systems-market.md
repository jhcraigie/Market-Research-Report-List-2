<p><p>Automobile Emission Control Systems market consists of various types of components like Oxygen Sensors, EGR Valves, Catalytic Converters, Air Pumps, PCV Valves, and Charcoal Canisters. These components work together to reduce harmful emissions released by vehicles, helping to protect the environment and improve air quality. Oxygen sensors monitor the oxygen levels in the exhaust gases, EGR valves recirculate exhaust gas to reduce nitrogen oxide emissions, catalytic converters convert harmful pollutants into less harmful substances, air pumps inject fresh air into the exhaust system, PCV valves control the flow of gases, and charcoal canisters capture fuel vapors to prevent them from being released into the atmosphere.</p></p>
