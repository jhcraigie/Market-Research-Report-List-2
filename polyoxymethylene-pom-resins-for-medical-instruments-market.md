<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM) resins are widely used in medical instruments due to their excellent mechanical properties, high strength, and chemical resistance. There are two main types of POM resins in the market - Homo-polyoxymethylene and Co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-POM resins are composed of only one type of monomer, while Co-POM resins are made by copolymerizing two different monomers. Both types of POM resins are essential for manufacturing a range of medical instruments such as surgical tools, catheters, and drug delivery devices.</p></p>
