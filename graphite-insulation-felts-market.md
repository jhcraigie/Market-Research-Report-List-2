<p><strong>The Graphite Insulation Felts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rayon Based</li><li>PAN Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphite insulation felts come in two main types: Rayon-based and PAN-based. Rayon-based felts are made from cellulose fibers, providing a high degree of flexibility and resilience. They are commonly used in high-temperature insulation applications. On the other hand, PAN-based felts are produced from polyacrylonitrile fibers, offering excellent thermal conductivity and insulation properties. They are often utilized in industries requiring high thermal stability and chemical resistance. Both types of graphite insulation felts offer unique advantages, catering to different application requirements.</p></p>
