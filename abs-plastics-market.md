<p><p>ABS plastics are categorized into three main market types: General Grade, Flame Retardant Grade, and Others. General Grade ABS plastics are the most commonly used type for various applications due to their balanced properties of toughness, impact resistance, and heat resistance. Flame Retardant Grade ABS plastics are specifically formulated to resist combustion and meet stringent fire safety standards. The Others category includes specialty grades tailored for specific applications, such as high heat resistance or enhanced aesthetics.</p></p>
