<p><p>Clear aligners are a type of dental alignment mouthpiece that are virtually invisible and provide a discreet way to straighten teeth. They are popular among adults and teenagers who prefer a more aesthetically pleasing alternative to traditional metal braces. On the other hand, conventional dental alignment mouthpieces consist of metal brackets and wires that are attached to the teeth to gradually shift them into the desired position. Both types of mouthpieces are effective in correcting dental alignment issues, but clear aligners are often favored for their cosmetic appeal.</p></p>
