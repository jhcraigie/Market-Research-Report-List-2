<p><strong>The Perovskite Photovoltaics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Structure</li><li>Inverted Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Perovskite photovoltaics can be categorized into two main market types based on their structure: normal structure and inverted structure. In normal structure perovskite photovoltaics, the perovskite layer is sandwiched between the electron transport layer and the hole transport layer, while in inverted structure perovskite photovoltaics, the order of the transport layers is reversed. Both market types offer advantages and disadvantages in terms of efficiency, stability, and production costs, catering to different consumer needs in the solar energy market.</p></p>
