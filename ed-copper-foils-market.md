<p><p>The ED Copper Foils market can be categorized into three types based on thickness: <20 μm, 20-50 μm, and >50 μm. Foils <20 μm are considered ultra-thin and are suitable for high-frequency circuits, RFID antennas, and flexible printed circuits. Foils in the 20-50 μm range are commonly used in mobile devices, laptops, and digital cameras. Foils >50 μm are thicker and are typically utilized in power electronics, automotive applications, and industrial equipment. Each type caters to specific requirements in various industries.</p></p>
