<p><p>Manual Strapping Machines are commonly divided into two main types: Welding Strapping Machines and Fastening Strapping Machines. Welding Strapping Machines use heat to join the strap ends together, providing a strong and secure seal. Fastening Strapping Machines, on the other hand, use buckles or clips to fasten the straps in place. Both types of machines are widely used in various industries for bundling and securing packages and goods for shipping or storage purposes.</p></p>
