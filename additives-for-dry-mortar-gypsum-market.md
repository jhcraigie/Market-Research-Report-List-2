<p><p>Additives for dry mortar and gypsum serve various purposes in construction projects. Defoamers are used to reduce foam formation, while superplasticizers improve the workability and strength of the mortar. Gypsum retarders control the setting time of gypsum-based materials, and foaming agents create air bubbles for improved insulation. Other additives include accelerators, water repellents, and shrinkage reducers. These additives play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and durability of dry mortar and gypsum products in construction applications.</p></p>
