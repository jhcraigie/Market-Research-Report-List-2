<p><ul><li>Hot-Rolled Steel Round Bars</li><li>Cold-Rolled Steel Round Bars</li><li>Forged Steel Round Bars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel round bars are available in different types in the market based on how they are manufactured. Hot-rolled steel round bars are produced by heating the steel at high temperatures and then rolling it into the desired shape. Cold-rolled steel round bars, on the other hand, are formed at lower temperatures, resulting in a smoother surface finish. Forged steel round bars are made by hammering or pressing the steel into shape, making them stronger and more durable than hot-rolled or cold-rolled bars.</p></p>
