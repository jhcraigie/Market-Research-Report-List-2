<p><p>Regular epidural sets and combined spinal epidural sets are two types of epidural anesthesia setups commonly used in medical settings. Regular epidural sets consist of a needle and catheter that deliver anesthesia directly to the epidural space, providing continuous pain relief during childbirth or surgery. Combined spinal epidural sets combine the benefits of both spinal and epidural anesthesia, offering rapid pain relief with the spinal injection and continuous relief with the epidural catheter. Both types of sets are essential tools in modern anesthesia practice.</p></p>
