<p><p>PV micro inverters are devices used in solar energy systems to convert DC power generated by solar panels into AC power for use in homes and businesses. There are two main types of PV micro inverter markets: standalone systems and integrated systems. Standalone systems involve individual inverters attached to each solar panel, offering more flexibility and easier maintenance. Integrated systems combine multiple panels into a single inverter, providing greater efficiency and cost savings. Both types of systems aim to maximize the energy output of solar installations.</p></p>
