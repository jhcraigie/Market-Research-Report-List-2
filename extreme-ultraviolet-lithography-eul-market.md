<p><p>Extreme ultraviolet lithography (EUL) market involves four main types: light source, mirror, mask, and others. The light source market involves the production and supply of the EUV radiation source used in the lithography process. The mirror market pertains to the highly specialized mirrors required to reflect the EUV light onto the silicon wafer. The mask market focuses on the development and manufacturing of masks containing the patterns to be transferred onto the wafer. The "others" category includes various auxiliary equipment and materials essential for the EUL process.</p></p>
