Metallized PP film is coated with a thin layer of metal to enhance its conductivity and performance in capacitors. BOPP film, on the other hand, is a more general-purpose film used in a variety of industries. Both types of films are essential in the production of high-quality capacitors for various electronic devices.</p></p>
