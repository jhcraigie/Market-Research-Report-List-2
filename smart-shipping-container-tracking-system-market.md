<p><p>Smart shipping container tracking systems are divided into two main market types: maritime tracking systems and land tracking systems. Maritime tracking systems focus on tracking containers while they are on ships, providing real-time location data to monitor the container's journey across the ocean. On the other hand, land tracking systems focus on tracking containers as they are transported over land, using GPS technology to monitor their movement on trucks, trains, or other modes of transportation. Both systems play a crucial role in optimizing supply chain operations and ensuring efficient delivery of goods.</p></p>
