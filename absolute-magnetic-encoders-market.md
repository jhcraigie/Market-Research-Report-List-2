<p><p>Absolute magnetic encoders are used for measuring linear or rotary motion with high accuracy. Linear magnetic encoders detect position changes in a linear path, while rotary magnetic encoders detect changes in a rotational path. Both types provide absolute position information, meaning they do not require a reference point for initialization. These encoders are commonly used in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and robotics to ensure precise positioning and movement control.</p></p>
