<p><strong>The Discrete Thyristors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unidirectional Thyristor</li><li>Bidirectional Thyristor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Discrete thyristors market consists of two main types: unidirectional thyristors and bidirectional thyristors. Unidirectional thyristors allow current flow in one direction only, making them suitable for applications such as rectifiers and inverters. On the other hand, bidirectional thyristors can conduct current in both directions, making them useful in applications requiring AC power control like dimmer switches. Both types of thyristors play crucial roles in various electrical and electronics systems, catering to different needs and applications.</p></p>
