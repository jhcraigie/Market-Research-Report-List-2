<p><strong>The Tufted Carpet Tile Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cut Pile</li><li>Loop Pile</li><li>Cut and Loop Pile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tufted carpet tiles come in various types, including cut pile, loop pile, and cut and loop pile. Cut pile carpets have loops cut to create individual tufts of yarn, resulting in a soft and plush texture. Loop pile carpets have uncut loops, making them durable and suitable for high-traffic areas. Cut and loop pile carpets combine both cut and looped fibers to create a textured appearance. Each type of tufted carpet tile offers unique benefits and aesthetics to suit different preferences and needs.</p></p>
