<p><strong>The Passenger Car Security Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immobilizer</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry (RKE)</li><li>Passive Keyless Entry (PKE)</li><li>Passive Keyless Go (PKG)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger car security systems market types include immobilizer, remote keyless entry (RKE), passive keyless entry (PKE), and passive keyless go (PKG). An immobilizer prevents the engine from starting without the correct key. RKE allows drivers to lock/unlock doors and start the engine remotely. PKE automatically unlocks doors when the key is in close proximity. PKG enables keyless starting of the engine and locking/unlocking of doors without pressing any buttons. These systems enhance convenience and security for vehicle owners.</p></p>
