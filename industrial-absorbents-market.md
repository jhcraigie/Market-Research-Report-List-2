<p><p>Industrial absorbents market can be categorized into three main types: natural inorganic absorbents, natural organic absorbents, and synthetic absorbents. Natural inorganic absorbents, such as clay and silica, are environmentally friendly and effective in absorbing liquids. Natural organic absorbents, like peat moss and sawdust, are also environmentally friendly and can absorb oils and chemicals. Synthetic absorbents, like polymers and cellulose, are manufactured materials that are highly absorbent and versatile. Each type offers different benefits and applications for industries requiring spill cleanup and containment solutions.</p></p>
