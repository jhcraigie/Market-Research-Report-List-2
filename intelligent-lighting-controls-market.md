<p><p>Intelligent Lighting Controls market includes two main types: Wired Technology and Wireless Technology. </p><p>Wired Technology involves direct physical connections between the lighting control devices, providing stable and reliable communication. This type of technology is commonly used in existing buildings or spaces where re-wiring is feasible.</p><p>Wireless Technology, on the other hand, uses radio frequency or infrared signals to communicate between devices, offering flexibility in installation and maintenance. It is popular in new constructions or retrofit projects where wiring may be challenging.</p></p>
