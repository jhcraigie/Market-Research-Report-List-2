<p><p>Automotive Mono Camera market types include CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras provide high-quality images with low noise levels, making them ideal for applications where image clarity is crucial. On the other hand, CMOS cameras are more energy-efficient and cost-effective, making them popular for mass production in automotive systems. Both types offer their own advantages and are widely used in advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) and other automotive applications.</p></p>
