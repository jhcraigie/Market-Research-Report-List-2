<p><p>Infra-red Sensor Faucet market is divided into two main types based on power source: Alternating Current (AC) Sensor Faucet and Direct Current (DC) Sensor Faucet. AC Sensor Faucets are powered by the building's electrical system and are more commonly used in commercial settings due to their stability and durability. On the other hand, DC Sensor Faucets are powered by batteries and are more flexible in terms of installation locations, making them popular for residential use. Both types offer touchless operation and water conservation benefits.</p></p>
