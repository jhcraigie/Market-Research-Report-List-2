<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Brake Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial vehicle brake fluids are classified into three main types: DOT 3, DOT 4, and DOT 5. DOT 3 brake fluids are glycol-based and commonly used in most commercial vehicles. DOT 4 brake fluids are also glycol-based but have higher boiling points and better performance under extreme conditions. DOT 5 brake fluids are silicone-based and are typically used in military and high-performance vehicles. Each type of brake fluid has specific characteristics and is designed to meet different requirements in commercial vehicle braking systems.</p></p>
