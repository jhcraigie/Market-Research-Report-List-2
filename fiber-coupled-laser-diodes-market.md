<p><p>Fiber coupled laser diodes are categorized into two main types: single mode and multimode. Single mode laser diodes emit a single wavelength of light with a narrow beam, ideal for precision applications such as telecommunications and medical procedures. On the other hand, multimode laser diodes emit multiple wavelengths with a wider beam, suitable for tasks like laser cutting and material processing. Both types cater to different industries and applications based on their specific characteristics and capabilities.</p></p>
