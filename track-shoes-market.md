<p><p>Track shoes market can be broadly divided into two main types: ordinary track shoes and spike shoes. Ordinary track shoes are designed with a flat sole and provide general traction for running on tracks. On the other hand, spike shoes have metal spikes or studs on the sole which provide enhanced traction and grip on the track surface, allowing athletes to run faster and have better control during races. Both types of track shoes cater to different needs and preferences of track and field athletes.</p></p>
