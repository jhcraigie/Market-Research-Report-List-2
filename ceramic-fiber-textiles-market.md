<p><ul><li>Cloth</li><li>Tape</li><li>Twisted Rope</li><li>Yarn</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic fiber textiles are available in various forms to cater to different industrial applications. Cloth is a flexible and durable material used for insulation and heat containment. Tape is a thin and flexible material used for wrapping pipes and sealing joints. Twisted rope is a thicker and more robust material used for gasketing and sealing applications. Yarn is a fine and lightweight material used for weaving fabrics or braiding into ropes. Each type of ceramic fiber textile offers unique properties and advantages based on its specific structure and composition.</p></p>
