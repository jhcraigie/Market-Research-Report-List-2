<p><p>Pharmaceutical plant extracts are available in two main types: non-standardized extracts and standardized extracts. Non-standardized extracts are prepared by using the whole plant, providing a variety of compounds that can vary in concentration. On the other hand, standardized extracts contain specific levels of active ingredients to ensure consistency and effectiveness. Non-standardized extracts are suitable for those looking for a more holistic approach, while standardized extracts are preferred by those seeking precise and predictable dosages for therapeutic purposes.</p></p>
