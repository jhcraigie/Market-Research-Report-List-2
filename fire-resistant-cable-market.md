<p><strong>The Fire Resistant Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>XPLE</li><li>LSZH</li><li>PVC</li><li>EPR</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire resistant cables are classified based on the materials used for insulation and sheathing. XPLE cables are cross-linked polyethylene cables that offer high resistance to fire. LSZH cables have a low smoke, zero halogen construction that limits the release of toxic fumes during a fire. PVC cables are flame retardant but emit toxic gases when burned. EPR cables use ethylene propylene rubber insulation for fire resistance. Other types of fire resistant cables may utilize different materials or construction techniques to provide protection against fire.</p></p>
