<p><p>Material handling automatic robotics machines come in various types such as articulated robots, SCARA robots, and parallel robots. Articulated robots have multiple joints allowing for flexible movement in multiple directions. SCARA robots have a horizontal joint and a vertical joint, providing high speed and precision for handling tasks. Parallel robots have multiple interconnected limbs giving them a high payload capacity and accuracy. Each type of robot caters to specific material handling needs and preferences in various industries.</p></p>
