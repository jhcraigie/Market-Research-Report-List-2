<p><p>Nuclear imaging market includes Positron Emission Tomography (PET), Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT), and Planar Scintigraphy Systems. PET utilizes radioactive tracers to create 3D images of the body's functions, while SPECT uses gamma rays to produce detailed 3D images. Planar scintigraphy systems create 2D images by detecting gamma rays emitted by a radioactive tracer. These imaging technologies are crucial for diagnosing and monitoring various medical conditions, including cancer, heart disease, and neurological disorders. The nuclear imaging market continues to expand with advancements in technology and increasing demand for accurate diagnostic tools.</p></p>
