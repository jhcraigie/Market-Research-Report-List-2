<p><p>Respiratory Protection Equipment (RPE) market includes various types such as Negative Pressure Air-Purifying Respirators (APR), Positive Pressure Powered Air-Purifying Respirators (PAPR), and Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA). Negative Pressure APR rely on the wearer's inhalation to draw air through the filter, while Positive Pressure PAPR use a powered fan to deliver filtered air. SCBA provides the highest level of respiratory protection as it supplies its own source of breathable air. These different types cater to varying levels of respiratory protection required in different industries.</p></p>
