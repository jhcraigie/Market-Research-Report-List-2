<p><p>The copper cable market can be divided into two main types: copper wire and copper cable. Copper wire refers to a single conductor made of copper, commonly used for electrical wiring. Copper cable, on the other hand, consists of multiple copper wires bundled together within a protective sheath, offering more durability and carrying capacity. Both copper wire and cable are essential components in various industries such as telecommunications, power distribution, and building construction.</p></p>
