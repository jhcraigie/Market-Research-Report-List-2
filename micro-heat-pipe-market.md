<p><p>Micro Heat Pipe Market Types consist of Pulsating Heat Pipe, Microgrooved Plate Heat Pipe, and Loop Heat Pipe. Pulsating Heat Pipe utilizes a two-phase flow mechanism to transfer heat efficiently. Microgrooved Plate Heat Pipe uses microgrooves on the inner surface to enhance capillary action and heat transfer. Loop Heat Pipe utilizes a wick structure to circulate the working fluid and transfer heat. These market types offer different approaches to heat transfer within constrained spaces, making them suitable for various applications in electronics cooling and thermal management.</p></p>
