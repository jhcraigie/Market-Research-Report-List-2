<p><p>Floor insulation materials are categorized into five main types: EPS/XPS, Rockwool, PIR/PU, Glasswool, and Others. EPS/XPS materials are lightweight and have good thermal properties. Rockwool is made of natural stone and recycled materials, providing fire resistance and sound insulation. PIR/PU materials are highly efficient and versatile. Glasswool is made from recycled glass and has good thermal and acoustic properties. Other materials include cork, hemp, and cotton, offering sustainable and eco-friendly options for floor insulation.</p></p>
