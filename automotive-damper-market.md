<p><p>The Automotive Damper Market can be classified into three main types: Hydraulic Type, Pneumatic Type, and Other types. Hydraulic dampers use fluid to absorb and dissipate energy, providing smooth and controlled movement. Pneumatic dampers use air pressure to achieve the same effect. The Other market segment includes damper technologies such as electromagnetic, friction, and rotary dampers. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in various automotive systems, catering to different requirements for performance and efficiency.</p></p>
